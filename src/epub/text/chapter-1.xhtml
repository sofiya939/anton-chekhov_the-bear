The Bear

A joke in one act.

Smirnov

To fight a duel! Now that’s what I call equality, that’s what I call emancipation! That is something I can respect! A mutual duel! Out of principle! I shall have the absolute pleasure of shooting you down like a bird! What kind of pistols do you wish to use?

Popova

My late husband’s pistols. I will fetch them here this instant. (Walks away hurriedly, then looks back.) What a sheer pleasure it will be to put a bullet straight through your thick skull! Wait here! (Exits.)
